Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

StrikeWave ("we," "us," "our") is a free streaming platform spanning Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, Gospel, tributes honoring the fallen and those who serve, and more — made available to everyone at no cost. This page explains what information is collected when you visit, and how it's used.

No accounts, no personal sign-up data

StrikeWave doesn't require an account, login, or email address to use. We don't collect names, passwords, or personal profiles from visitors.

Advertising & cookies

StrikeWave is free to use because it's supported by advertising. We use Google AdSense to display ads, which may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Show ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info.

Audio hosting

Music files and cover art are streamed from Cloudinary, a third-party media hosting service. When you play a track, your device connects directly to Cloudinary's servers to stream that file.

Analytics

We may use basic, aggregated analytics (such as page views) to understand how StrikeWave is used and to improve it. This data is not used to personally identify individual visitors.

Children's privacy

StrikeWave is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

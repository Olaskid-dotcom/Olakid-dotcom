Behind StrikeWave
The founder
Amusan Olamilekan
Founder, StrikeWave

Amusan Olamilekan is the founder and driving force behind StrikeWave — a platform built to give voice to the sounds that move people, and to honor those who serve with quiet courage. Born and raised in Ogun State, Nigeria, he carries forward values of discipline, loyalty, and respect into everything StrikeWave stands for. His vision was simple: build a space where music tells the full story — the rhythm of everyday life alongside the tribute owed to the fallen and the brave. StrikeWave exists because he believed no one should have to pay, sign up, or wait to press play.
